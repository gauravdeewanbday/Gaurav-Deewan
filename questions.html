Newtonian Motion Challenge

1. A body is thrown vertically upward with a speed of 20 m/s. How long will it take to return to the thrower's hand? (g = 10 m/s²)

2. A car accelerates from rest at 2 m/s². What is the distance covered in the 5th second?

3. A 5 kg object is pushed with a force of 20 N. What is the acceleration?

4. Two blocks of mass 2 kg and 4 kg are connected by a light string and pulled with 18 N on a frictionless surface. What is the tension in the string between them?

5. A lift accelerates upward at 2 m/s². What is the apparent weight of a 50 kg man inside it? (g = 10 m/s²)
